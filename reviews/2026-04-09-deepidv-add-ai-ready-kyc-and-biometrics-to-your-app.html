Lab Report

deepidv

One platform for identity verification, deepfake detection, and real-time compliance. Powered by agentic AI, built to drop into your existing auth stack.

Try It

Verdict

deepidv is built for startups and mid-stage companies that need production-grade KYC without stitching together three or four separate vendors. If you are already running Firebase, Clerk, or a similar auth layer and need to layer on document verification, liveness checks, or AML screening, this is a compelling single-API solution. The free first month and pay-per-use entry point make it low-risk to validate, though you will want to model your per-check costs carefully before scaling past the trial phase.

Pricing

Two tiers. No minimum commitments on either. Cancel anytime.

Key Features

What you actually get under the hood.

🔍

Real-Time ID Verification

Document scanning, data extraction, and authenticity checks processed in seconds. Supports passports, driver's licenses, and national IDs across multiple countries.

🛡️

Deepfake Detection

AI-powered analysis across images, video, and audio to flag synthetic or manipulated media during verification flows. This is increasingly table-stakes for fintech and crypto onboarding.

👤

Face Liveness + Match

Biometric liveness detection confirms a real person is present. Face match then compares the live capture against the submitted ID document photo.

🤖

Agentic AI Monitoring

Continuous, autonomous monitoring that flags risk changes post-onboarding. This goes beyond one-time verification into ongoing compliance territory.

⚙️

Customizable Verification Flows

Build multi-step verification sequences tailored to your risk profile. Choose which checks to include, set thresholds, and define fallback logic.

🔗

Broad SDK + Integration Support

Native SDKs for iOS, Android, React Native, and Flutter. Web SDKs for JavaScript, Next.js, and React. Plus Zapier and a full Open API for custom backends.

📋

All-in-One Screening Suite

AML screening, sanctions list checks, PEP identification, and background checks bundled into the same platform. Reduces vendor sprawl significantly.

📊

Risk Scoring

Composite risk scores generated from verification results, screening data, and behavioral signals. Useful for automated decisioning in onboarding pipelines.

Who Should Use This

deepidv is not a general-purpose tool. Here is where it fits best.

Fintech Startups

If you are building a neobank, lending platform, or payment app and need to meet regulatory KYC/AML requirements without hiring a compliance engineering team, deepidv can serve as your verification backbone. The unlimited plan at $129/seat makes cost predictable.

Crypto and Web3 Platforms

Exchanges and DeFi on-ramps facing tightening regulations need identity verification that can catch deepfakes and synthetic identities. The deepfake detection layer is a genuine differentiator here.

Marketplaces and Gig Platforms

Platforms that need to verify service providers, sellers, or freelancers before granting access. Background checks and identity verification in one flow reduce onboarding friction and fraud risk simultaneously.

SaaS with Regulated Data

Healthcare, legal tech, or any SaaS product handling sensitive user data where identity assurance is a compliance requirement. The ability to layer deepidv on top of existing Clerk or Firebase auth means you do not have to rip out your current stack.

Limitations

What to watch out for before you commit.

  • ⚠ Opaque per-check pricing. The Pay-Per-Use tier does not publicly list what each verification, liveness check, or screening call costs. You are flying blind until you request a rate card or run test transactions. For a product targeting developers, this is a friction point.
  • ⚠ New product, limited track record. deepidv is freshly launched on Product Hunt. There is no public information about uptime SLAs, processing volume benchmarks, or enterprise case studies. If you are in a heavily regulated industry, you may need to do extra due diligence on their compliance certifications and data handling practices.
  • ⚠ Per-seat pricing on the unlimited plan. $129/month per seat is reasonable for a small team, but it scales linearly. A compliance team of 10 is $1,290/month before you even factor in engineering time. Compare this to competitors like Onfido or Veriff, which price by verification volume rather than seats.
  • ⚠ Integration required for full value. This is not a no-code plug-and-play solution. You need engineering resources to integrate the SDKs or API. The Zapier integration helps for simpler workflows, but the real power is in the API, which means developer time.
  • ⚠ Country and document coverage unclear. The site does not specify exactly which countries and document types are supported. For global products, this is critical information that you will need to verify directly with the team before building around it.

Ready to try deepidv?

The free first month on Pay-Per-Use means you can validate the integration with zero financial risk. Worth testing if KYC is on your roadmap.
