Lab Report

Pensieve

Capture ideas quickly and remember them with a notes app designed for people who actually think fast. Sync across devices, organize with ease, and stop losing your best ideas.

Try Pensieve

The Verdict

Pensieve is a lightweight, fast-capture notes app built for creatives, multitaskers, and people with ADHD who need to externalize thoughts before they vanish. It is not the organizational knowledge graph for AI agents that its recent marketing suggests. If you need a frictionless personal note-taking tool with cross-device sync, the free tier is worth trying. If you came here expecting to plug it into your AI agent stack as a company-wide context layer, you will be disappointed; the product's actual feature set does not match that promise yet.

Key Features

bolt

Quick Capture

Designed for speed. Open the app, dump your thought, move on. The interface is deliberately minimal to reduce friction between having an idea and recording it.

sync

Cross-Device Sync

Notes sync across your devices so you can capture on your phone and review on your laptop. Standard but essential for a tool in this category.

perm_media

Multiple Media Formats

Supports text, images, and other media types within notes. Useful for creatives who think in mixed formats rather than pure text.

notifications_active

Reminders

Set reminders on captured notes so ideas do not just sit in a graveyard. Particularly valuable for ADHD users who capture prolifically but struggle with follow-through.

checklist

Task Lists

Built-in task list functionality bridges the gap between "idea captured" and "action taken." Lightweight compared to dedicated task managers, but sufficient for personal workflows.

touch_app

Minimal UI

The interface is clean and intentionally simple. No nested menus, no complex organizational hierarchies. This is a feature, not a limitation, for its target audience.

Who Should Use This

Writers and Creators

If you constantly have fragments of ideas, dialogue snippets, or visual references that need a home, Pensieve's quick capture is built for you. It is not a writing tool; it is a pre-writing tool.

People with ADHD

Pensieve explicitly targets this audience, and it shows. The low-friction capture, reminders, and simple organization reduce the cognitive overhead that makes most note apps feel like chores.

Multitaskers on the Go

If you are switching between meetings, commutes, and conversations and need to capture thoughts in seconds, the mobile-first design makes sense. Cross-device sync means nothing gets stranded on one device.

People Overwhelmed by Notion/Obsidian

If you have tried complex note systems and abandoned them because the setup cost was too high, Pensieve's simplicity is the point. It trades power for consistency of use.

Limitations

  • warning
    The marketing does not match the product. Pensieve has been positioned in some channels as an organizational knowledge graph that gives AI agents full company context. The actual product is a personal note-taking app. There is no evidence of a knowledge graph, AI agent integration, or connections to Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google inference on the current website. If you came here for that use case, this is not the tool.
  • warning
    Opaque pricing. The paid tier exists but the price is not listed publicly. You have to create an account to find out what "All Access" costs. This is frustrating and unnecessary.
  • warning
    Limited free tier. The free version covers basics, but advanced features and integrations are locked behind the subscription. Without knowing the price, it is hard to evaluate whether the upgrade is worth it.
  • warning
    Platform availability gaps. Not available on all platforms. If your workflow spans Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, and web, verify that Pensieve covers your specific combination before committing.
  • warning
    Shallow integrations. The listed integrations (email clients, productivity apps, task management tools) are vague. There is no specific mention of Slack, Notion, Todoist, or any named tool. This makes it difficult to assess how well Pensieve fits into an existing stack.

Ready to try Pensieve?

The free tier costs nothing. If you need a dead-simple capture tool, it is worth 10 minutes of your time. Just do not expect an AI agent context layer.
