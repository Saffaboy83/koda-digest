TIP 01 — Mindset
Don't Let AI Become Your Exoskeleton
GPT-5.4 and Claude Opus 4.6 can do your thinking for you. That is exactly the problem. Use AI to accelerate your thinking, not replace it. Feed your analysis to the model. Don't ask the model for your analysis. The exoskeleton effect is real: professionals who outsource judgment to AI lose the muscle over time. Keep the hard thinking in your head. Use AI for execution and research.
